Client Setup
To start, visit the Chrome Webstore to download this SSH app and open it up.
Next, type your username and hostname in the respective fields.
Then put -D 8800 in the SSH Arguments field. Then press Enter and login to the Pi with your password.
NOTE: YOU MUST LEAVE THIS TAB OPEN IN ORDER FOR THE SSH TUNNEL TO CONTINUE WORKING.

Next, you're going to want to download my Chrome extension.
Then, open a new tab and type chrome://extensions.
Now open the file browser on your operating system, whether that's Explorer, Finder, etc, and drag the LSVPN.crx from your Downloads folder onto the Extensions page. This will install the extension on your Chrome browser.
NOTE: IF YOU ARE ON WINDOWS OR MAC, YOU WILL HAVE TO INSTALL THE LSVPN EXTENSION EVERY TIME YOU CLOSE CHROME.
